Best Traditional Lip-Smacking Chanachur

Durga Mata Chanachur is a household name in West Bengal, known for delivering the best in class namkeen, chanachur, and snacks for over two decades. Established in 1998, our company has been in the business of producing and delivering high-quality snacks to customers across the state.

Quality is our top priority:
We use only the finest ingredients to make our chanachur.

Taste that you can't resist:
Our chanachur has a taste that you can't resist, thanks to our years of experience and perfectly balanced flavors.

Keeping traditions alive:
We keep traditions alive by preserving age-old recipes and delivering authentic, traditional chanachur to our customers.

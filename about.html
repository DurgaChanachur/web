Durga Mata Chanachur

Delivering the Best in Class Namkeen and Chanachur Since 1998

Durga Mata Chanachur is a household name in West Bengal, known for delivering the best in class namkeen, chanachur, and snacks for over two decades. Established in 1998, our company has been in the business of producing and delivering high-quality snacks to customers across the state. Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a favorite among snack lovers, and we take pride in our wide range of offerings that cater to all tastes and preferences

About Us

Durga Mata Chanachur is a company that is built on a legacy of quality, trust, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to delivering the best in class namkeen, chanachur, and snacks to our customers, and we take great pride in the products that we produce

Our Products

We offer a wide range of products, including chanachur, mixture, and namkeen, all of which are made with the finest ingredients and cooked to perfection. Our products are available in various sizes and packages to suit the needs of our customers. We take pride in the quality of our products, which are made with the utmost care and attention to detail.

Our Legacy

Durga Mata Chanachur is one of the oldest companies in Howrah, West Bengal, and has been delivering the best in class namkeen and chanachur for over two decades. Our legacy is built on the trust and loyalty of our customers, who have been with us since the beginning. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us build a reputation that is unmatched in the industry.

Our Promise

At Durga Mata Chanachur, we are committed to delivering the best in class namkeen and chanachur to our customers. We use only the finest ingredients and state-of-the-art equipment to produce our snacks. Our team of experts ensures that every product that leaves our facility meets our strict quality standards. We believe that our customers deserve the very best, and that is what we strive to deliver every day.

Our Delivery

We offer delivery services across West Bengal, ensuring that our customers can enjoy our products no matter where they are. Our delivery process is efficient and reliable, and we take great care to ensure that our products reach our customers in the best possible condition. We also offer packaging solutions that are designed to keep our products fresh and flavorful for longer.
